12. Platform load greater 1200 lbs/544 kg or less.  Power relays disengaged.   Remove weight from platform until load is than 1250 lbs/567 kg
13. CAN communication failure   Power relays disengaged.   Repair CAN bus wiring and/or connections.
14. Sensor power failure   Power relays disengaged.   Use recovery switch and APU from the ground controls to recover the machine to stowed position or within the operating envelope.
15. Value too low   Power relays disengaged. String potentiometer wiring open or shorted to ground.  Repair wiring and/or connections OR replace string potentiometer and calibrate overload system.
16. Output short low   Power relays disengaged. String potentiometer wiring open or shorted to ground.  Repair wiring and/or connections OR replace string potentiometer and calibrate overload system.

18. Just calibrated   Used to clear diagnostic codes.  Self-clearing.
