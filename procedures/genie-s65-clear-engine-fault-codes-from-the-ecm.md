# Clear Engine Fault Codes from the ECM

**Equipment:** Genie S-60 / S-65 / S-60 TRAX / S-65 TRAX

1. Open the engine side turntable cover and locate the battery.
2. Disconnect the negative battery cable from the battery for a minimum of 5 minutes.  Electrocution/burn hazard. Contact with electrically charged circuits could result in death or serious injury. Remove all rings, watches and other jewelry.
3. Connect the negative battery cable to the battery.


Service and Repair Manual   September 2016  Ford MSG-425 Engine Fault Codes
16. Never crank synced at start

> **Note:** Perform this procedure with the engine off and the key switch in the off position.
