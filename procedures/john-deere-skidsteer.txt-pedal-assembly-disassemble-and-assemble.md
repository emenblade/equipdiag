# Pedal Assembly Disassemble and Assemble

**Equipment:** John Deere 326D / 328D / 329D / 332D / 333D

1. Remove pedal assembly. See Pedal Assembly Remove and Install. (Group 3115.)
2. Remove cotter pin (4), shaft (6) and bushings (3) from pedal (5).
3. Inspect shaft and bushings for wear. Replace parts as necessary.
4. Remove nut (1) and pedal from linkage rod (2).
