# Remove the Boom Extension Cylinders

**Equipment:** Genie S-80 / S-85

1. Raise the boom to a horizontal position, fully extend the boom and fully lower the platform.
1. Raise the boom to a horizontal position, fully extend the boom and fully lower the platform.

> **Note:** When removing a hose assembly or fitting, the O-ring (if equipped) on the fitting and/or the hose end must be replaced.

> **Note:** Note the length of the cylinder after removal.

> **Note:** During re-assembly of the boom, fully retract the boom after installing all pins.
