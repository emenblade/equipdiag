# Engine Compartment Relays and Fuses

**Equipment:** JLG G5-18A

1. Engine Compartment Relays and Fuses  There are seven relays and one buss bar located on the frame, mounted on a bracket behind the battery. The relays control the auxiliary ( 1 ), ignition ( 2 ), fuel pump ( 3 ), Cut Out ( 4 ), Auxiliary ( 5 ) and Joystick ( 6   and   7 ).  9.
