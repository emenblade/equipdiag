# Replacing the Thermal Relay

**Equipment:** Doosan DGK25B / 45A / 45C / 60A / 70B / 100B

1. Gain access to the internal control panel.
2. Disconnect all leads connected to the thermal relay. Refer to page 24 (DGK25A, DGK25B) or page 25 (DGK45A, DGK60A, and DGK70B), Page 26 (DGK45C), or Page 27 (DGK100B) Internal Control Panel Layout for the location of the thermal relay.
3. Loosen and remove the 2 screws on the lower side of the relay and remove the Thermal Relay.
