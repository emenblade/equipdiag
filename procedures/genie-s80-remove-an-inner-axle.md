# Remove an Inner Axle

**Equipment:** Genie S-80 / S-85

1. Extend the axles.
2. Remove the drive chassis cover. 3   2WD models:   Remove the yoke and hub assembly. See 11-1, How to Remove the Yoke and Hub.  4WD models:   Remove the yoke and drive hub. See 12-1, How to Remove the Yoke and Drive Hub Assembly. 4   4WD models:   Remove the hose bracket from the steer cylinder barrel-end pivot pin.
5. Remove the remaining steer cylinder pivot pin. Remove the steer cylinder and lay off to the side.
6. Remove the steer cylinder mounting lug from the inner axle.
7. Place blocks under the axle extension cylinder for support.
8. Remove the external snap ring from the axle extension cylinder pivot pin. Use a soft metal drift to remove the pin.

> **Note:** When removing a hose assembly or fitting, the O-ring on the fitting and/or hose end must be replaced and then torqued to specification during installation.

> **Note:** Perform this procedure with the boom in the stowed position and boom between the non-steer end tires.

> **Note:** Pay careful attention to the quantity and location of shims used when removing the wear pads.

> **Note:** Pay careful attention to the location and number of shims used when removing the wear pads.
