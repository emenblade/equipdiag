# Remove the Hydraulic Tank

**Equipment:** Genie GR-15 / GR-20

1. Tag, disconnect and plug the hydraulic supply hard line at the pump. Cap the fitting on the pump. Remove the hard line from the tank.  Hydraulic Tank
2. Tag, disconnect and plug the return hard line at the hydraulic filter. Cap the fitting on the filter. Bodily injury hazard. Spraying hydraulic oil can penetrate and burn skin. Loosen hydraulic connections very slowly to allow the oil pressure to dissipate gradually. Do not allow oil to squirt or spray.

> **Note:** When removing a hose assembly or fitting, the fitting and/or hose end must be torqued to specification during installation.

> **Note:** Perform this procedure with the platform in the stowed position.
