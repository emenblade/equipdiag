# Shim an Extendable Axle

**Equipment:** Genie S-80 / S-85

1. Center a lifting jack of sufficient capacity under the outer axle that needs to be shimmed. Do not raise the machine.
2. Loosen the side wear pad mounting fasteners on the outer axle.
3. Install the new shims under the wear pads to obtain zero clearance and zero drag.
4. Use a round punch to align the shim to the wear pad. Tighten the mounting fasteners.
5. Loosen the wear pad mounting fasteners from the top and bottom wear pads on both the inner and outer axles.
6. Install the new shims under the wear pads of the inner and outer axle to obtain zero clearance and zero drag.
7. Use a round punch through the wear pad mounting holes to align the shims with the wear pad. Install the wear pad retaining fasteners.

> **Note:** Replace the pad if thickness is less than minimum   specification.

> **Note:** Always maintain squareness between the inner and outer axle tubes.
