# Remove the Platform Controls Circuit Board

**Equipment:** Genie GR-15 / GR-20

> **Electrocution/burn hazard.**
>
1. Push in the red Emergency Stop button to the off position at both the ground and platform controls.
2. Remove the retaining fasteners securing the bottom to the platform control box. Open the control box.
3. Remove the ties securing the wire harness.
4. Remove the alarm and red Emergency Stop button from the platform control box.
5. Carefully disconnect the three wire harness connectors from the platform controls circuit board.
6. Carefully remove the platform controls circuit board retaining fasteners.

> **Note:** Select a test area that is firm, level and free of obstructions.
