# LSI Modules

**Equipment:** JLG G5-18A

1. Remove any fork carrier or attachment from the machine.
2. Park the machine on a firm, level surface, level the machine, fully retract the boom, fully raise the boom, place the transmission in (N) NEUTRAL, engage the park brake and shut the engine OFF.
3. Place a Do Not Operate Tag on both the ignition key switch and the steering wheel, stating that the machine should not be operated.
4. LSI Modules  The LSI modules ( 15 ) are bolted to the frame at the inside left rear corner.  a.   LSI Module Removal
5. The LEDs will perform a rolling sequence. When only the third green LED illuminates ( 3 ), press the SYSTEM CHECK button ( 6 ).
6. Disconnect the LSI modules electrical connectors.
7. Loosen and remove the two bolts holding the LSI modules to the side of the frame.
8. Remove each module.  DO NOT   disassemble the module(s). The module(s) are not serviceable. Replace module(s) if found to be defective.  MAM2910
