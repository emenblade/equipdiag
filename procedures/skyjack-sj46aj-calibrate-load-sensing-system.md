# Calibrate the Load Sensing System

**Equipment:** Skyjack SJ46AJ / SJ51AJ

1. Ensure the aerial platform is on firm level ground.
2. Fully retract and lower the boom.
3. Turn the engine off.
4. Ensure both emergency stop buttons are pulled out and the platform engine enable switch is in the ON position.
5. Ensure the platform is unloaded and free from any surface contact.
6. Remove the cap from the load cell interface connector.
7. Connect the "Teach In" handset to the interface connector.
8. On the "Teach In" handset, depress button 4 for four seconds.
9. Add a test load of 244 kg onto the center of the platform.
10. Depress button 4 for four seconds.
11. Disconnect the "Teach In" handset from the interface connector.
12. Wait four seconds, then add a test load exceeding 244 kg to test the load sensing system.
13. Remove the test load from the platform.
14. Recap the interface connector and place the provided "CALIBRATED" label over the interface connector.

> **Note:** Do not interrupt system power during this procedure.

> **Note:** After step 7, the T label on the handset will flash at 5 Hz. After step 8, the T label will light constantly then flash at 1.25 Hz. After step 10, the T label will go from flashing to constantly on.

> **Note:** After calibration, the green power LED should be flashing, the orange LED should be ON, the red alarm LED should be OFF, and the red error LED should be OFF.
