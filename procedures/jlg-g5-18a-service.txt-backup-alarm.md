# Backup Alarm

**Equipment:** JLG G5-18A

1. Backup Alarm  The back-up alarm is located at the rear of the machine.When the park brake is released and the transmission shift control switch (transmission) is shifted to the (R) REVERSE position, the back-up alarm will automatically sound.  a.   Disassembly DO NOT   disassemble the back-up alarm. Replace a defective or faulty alarm with a new part.  b.   Inspection and Replacement  Inspect the wiring harness connector and alarm terminals for continuity and shorting. Test the alarm by turning the ignition key switch to the RUN position and shifting the transmission into the REVERSE position. The alarm should sound.
