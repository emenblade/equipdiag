# Air Cleaner Assembly Removal

**Equipment:** JLG G5-18A

1. Air Cleaner Assembly Removal
2. Place a Do Not Operate Tag on both the ignition key switch and steering wheel.
3. Open the engine cover. Allow the system fluids to cool.
5. Loosen the clamp bolt and remove air intake adapter duct ( 1 ) from air cleaner assembly ( 2 ).
6. Loosen clamp ( 3 ) securing air intake elbow to air cleaner assembly. Pull the air intake elbow off the air cleaner.
7. Remove the hardware securing the air cleaner to the air cleaner mounting plate ( 4 ). Remove the air cleaner assembly ( 2 ).  7.
