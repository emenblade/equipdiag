# Calibration To calibrate the LSI

**Equipment:** JLG G5-18A

1. With the estimated test weight on the forks, start the machine and extend the boom horizontally until the machine starts to tip. This point should be at an extension of Xtip ( 1 ) of the appropriate boom section. If the machine is not tipping at this extension, weight needs to be added or removed from the forks.
2. Pressing the LSI override button ( 8 ) up to 30 seconds may be required to reach tipping point.
3. By confirming that the machine tips at this point, the correct amount of weight is now on the forks.
4. The machine control system must be powered on for at least 10 minutes before calibration.
5. The machine must be on a level surface with the wheels steered straight, boom retracted and park brake OFF, with standard carriage, forks and test weight attached to the machine.
6. Drive the machine forward over a distance of at least 2 m (6.5 ft) before entering a calibration point.  Note:   If machine is equipped with outriggers, continue to step 7. If machine is NOT equipped with outriggers, proceed to step 8.
7. Detach standard carriage, forks and test weight at a minimum of distance of X place ( 3 ) in front of the machine. Pressing LSI override button ( 16 ) may be required.
8. Fully retract and level the boom. Fully lower the outriggers and shut engine OFF. Pressing the LSI override button ( 16 ) may be required. Do Not move machine.
