# Towing Procedure

**Equipment:** JLG G5-18A

1. Securely block all four wheels.
2. Towing Procedure
3. Connect an auxiliary hydraulic power supply to P1 port ( 3 ) on front axle and apply 220 - 435 psi (15 - 30 bar) to release the park brake.  Note: DO NOT   apply more than 450 psi (31 bar) to release the park brake. Excessive pressure could damage the axle brake system.
4. Loosen unlocking bolts ( 4 ) located at front and back side of axle housing and remove stop washers.
5. Tighten unlocking bolts against axle housing.
