# Axle Removal

**Equipment:** JLG G5-18A

1. Park machine on a firm, level surface, level machine, fully retract boom, lower boom, place transmission in (N) NEUTRAL, engage park brake and shut engine OFF.
2. Place a Do Not Operate Tag on both ignition key switch and steering wheel.
3. Open engine cover. Allow system fluids to cool.
5. If the axle will be disassembled after removal, the oil will have to be drained. Loosen the breather to release possible internal pressure, then tighten breather with a torque wrench to 9 lb-ft (12 Nm).  WARNING  An improperly supported machine can fall, causing death or severe personal injury. Safely raise and adequately support the machine so that it will remain stable and in place before attempting to remove an axle.  MAE32810  4 5
7. Label, disconnect and cap steering and brake lines at axle. Wipe up any spilled oil.
8. Block front and rear of both tires on axle that is not being removed. Ensure that machine will remain in place during axle removal before proceeding.
