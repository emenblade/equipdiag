# Assembly Instructions for Flange Connections (FL61 and FL62)

**Equipment:** JLG G5-18A

1. Ensure sealing surfaces are free of rust, splits, scratches, dirt, foreign matter, or burrs.
2. Pre-lubricate the o-ring with hydraulic oil.
3. Position flange and clamp halves.
4. Place lock washers on bolt and bolt through clamp halves.
5. Tighten all bolts by hand.
