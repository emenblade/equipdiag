# Remove the Flex Plate

**Equipment:** Genie S-80 / S-85

1. Open the engine side turntable cover.
2. Tag and disconnect the battery cables from the battery(s). Electrocution hazard. Contact with electrically charged circuits could result in death or serious injury. Remove all rings, watches and other jewelry.
3. Remove the engine tray retaining fastener. Swing the engine tray out away from the machine. a   engine tray anchor hole b   engine tray retaining fastener
5. Tag and disconnect the wiring from the bell housing.
6. Remove the U-bolt from the exhaust flex pipe at the muffler. Burn hazard. Hot engine parts can cause severe burns.
7. Remove the muffler bracket retaining fasteners from bell housing. Remove the muffler and bracket assembly from the engine.
8. Support the drive pump with an appropriate lifting device. Remove all of the remaining bell housing engine fasteners.

> **Note:** Perform this procedure with the engine off and cool to the touch.
