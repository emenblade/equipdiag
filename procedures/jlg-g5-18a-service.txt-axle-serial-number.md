# AXLE SERIAL NUMBER

**Equipment:** JLG G5-18A

3. AXLE SERIAL NUMBER  The front and rear axle serial number plate is located on the inside of each axle on the right side of the center section. Information on the serial number plate is required in correspondence regarding the axle. Supply information from the axle serial number plate when communicating about an axle assembly or axle components.
