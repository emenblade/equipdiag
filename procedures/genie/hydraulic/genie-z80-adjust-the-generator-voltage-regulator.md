# Adjust the Generator Voltage Regulator

**Equipment:** Genie Z-80 / Z-80/60

> **Note:** Perform this test only on models equipped with an oscillating axle.

> **Note:** Before replacing the hydraulic oil, the oil may be tested by an oil distributor for specific levels of contamination to verify that changing the oil is necessary.

> **Note:** If the hydraulic oil was not replaced at or before the 2000 hour maintenance interval, the oil must be tested every quarter by an oil distributor until the oil fails the test and is replaced.

> **Note:** When replacing the hydraulic oil, it is recommended that all hydraulic filters be replaced at the same time.

> **Note:** There are four types of hydraulic filters: tank return filter, medium pressure filter, high pressure filter and drive motor case drain filter.

> **Note:** Models without a filter condition indicator cannot be inspected.

> **Note:** When checking for positive tank cap venting, air should pass freely through the cap.

> **Note:** These procedures do not apply to the following engine models.

> **Note:** Some procedures may require two people.

> **Note:** For engine RPM information refer to Specifications , Engine Specifications   for your engine model.

> **Note:** Use the following chart to identify the description of each LCD screen control button used in this procedure.

2. Set the engine idle to high rpm (rabbit symbol).
