# Test the Hydraulic Pump When removing a hose assembly or fi

**Equipment:** Genie 1932 / 2032 / 2046

1. Tag, disconnect and plug the high pressure hydraulic hose from the hydraulic pump.
2. Connect a 0 to 5000 psi / 0 to 350 bar pressure gauge to the high pressure port on the pump.
3. Turn the key switch to ground control and pull out the red Emergency Stop button to the on position at both ground and platform controls.
4. Activate the platform up function from the ground controls.
5. Remove the pressure gauge and reconnect the hydraulic hose. Torque to specifications.
