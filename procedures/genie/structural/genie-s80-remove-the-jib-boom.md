# Remove the Jib Boom

**Equipment:** Genie S-80 / S-85

> **Note:** Perform this procedure with the boom in the stowed position.

> **Note:** When removing a hose assembly or fitting, the O-ring (if equipped) on the fitting and/or the hose end must be replaced.

> **Note:** Models equipped with platform overoad system:   If the platform overload components are disassembled and/or removed from the platform support, the platform overload system will need to be calibrated.

1. Remove the platform. See 2-1, How to Remove the Platform. Note:   Models equipped with platform overoad system:   If the platform overload components are disassembled and/or removed from the platform support, the platform overload system will need to be calibrated. See 2-4, How to Calibrate the Platform Overload System.
2. Models equipped with platform overload system:   Tag and disconnect the electrical connector from the platform load sense module.
3. Remove the hose and cable cover from the side of the jib boom.
4. Remove the hose and cable clamp from the jib boom pivot pin. Lay all hoses and cables to the side.
5. Tag, disconnect and plug the jib boom lift cylinder hydraulic hoses. Cap the fittings on the cylinder.
6. Attach a lifting strap from an overhead crane to the jib boom assembly.
7. Place blocks under the platform leveling cylinder for support. Protect the cylinder rod from damage.
8. Remove the pin retaining fastener from the slave cylinder rod-end pivot pin. Use a soft metal drift to remove the pin. Crushing hazard. The jib boom could fall when the slave cylinder rod-end pivot pin is removed if not properly attached to the overhead crane.
