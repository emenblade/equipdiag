# Remove the Drive Pump

**Equipment:** Genie S-80 / S-85

> **Note:** When removing a hose assembly or fitting, the O-ring (if equipped) on the fitting and/or the hose end must be replaced.

> **Note:** Before installing the pump, verify proper pump coupler spacing.

1. Disconnect the wire harness at the electronic displacement controller (EDC), located on the drive pump.
3. Tag, disconnect and plug the hydraulic hoses from the drive and function pumps. Cap the fittings on the pumps.
4. Support the pumps with a suitable lifting device and remove the two drive pump mounting fasteners.
5. Carefully pull the drive pump out until the pump coupler separates from the flex plate.
6. Remove the drive pump assembly from the machine.
