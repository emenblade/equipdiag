# Remove a Steer Cylinder There are two identical steer cylin

**Equipment:** Genie S-80 / S-85

> **Note:** When removing a hose assembly or fitting, the O-ring (if equipped) on the fitting and/or the hose end must be replaced.

1. Tag, disconnect and plug the hydraulic hoses from the steer cylinder. Cap the fittings on the cylinder.
2. Remove the pin retaining fasteners from the steer cylinder pivot pins. Remove the pivot pin from each end of the steer cylinder.
