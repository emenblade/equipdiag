# Boom Wear Pad Lubrication

**Equipment:** JLG G5-18A

3. Boom Wear Pad Lubrication  After replacing any wear pads or after prolonged periods of inoperation,   Lubrication of boom wear surfaces with grease is recommended to keep boom wear surfaces lubricated properly. Lubrication of boom wear surfaces is also recommended when machine is stored, to help prevent rusting. Refer to Section 2.3, “Fluids, Lubricants and Capacities”.
