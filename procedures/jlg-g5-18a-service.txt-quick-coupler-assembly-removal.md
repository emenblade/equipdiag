# Quick Coupler Assembly Removal

**Equipment:** JLG G5-18A

1. Quick Coupler Assembly Removal
2. Place a Do Not Operate Tag on both ignition key switch and steering wheel.
4. Open engine cover. Allow system fluids to cool.
5. If equipped with a hydraulic quick coupler device, remove both hydraulic hoses ( 1 ) from valve on left side of boom head. Plug and cap hose ends to prevent dirt and debris from entering system.
6. Remove the lock bolt ( 2 ) holding tilt cylinder rod end pin ( 3 ) to quick attach assembly. Remove pin.
7. Support quick coupler assembly. Remove lock bolt ( 4 ) holding quick attach pin ( 5 ) to quick attach assembly. Remove pin.
8. Inspect pins for nicks or surface corrosion. Use fine emery cloth to fix minor nicks or corrosion.
