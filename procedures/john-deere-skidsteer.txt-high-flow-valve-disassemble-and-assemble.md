# High Flow Valve Disassemble and Assemble

**Equipment:** John Deere 326D / 328D / 329D / 332D / 333D

1. Park machine on a flat level surface.
2. Remove high flow valve (1). See High Flow Valve Remove and Install. (Group 2160.)
3. Remove straight fitting (2) and run tee fitting (3) from high flow valve.
4. Remove pressure relief valve (4) from high flow valve.
5. Remove nut (6) and solenoid valve coil (5) from solenoid valve (7).
6. Remove solenoid valve from high flow valve.
7. Clean and inspect parts. Repair or replace parts as necessary.
