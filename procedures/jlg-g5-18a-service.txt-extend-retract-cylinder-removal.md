# Extend/Retract Cylinder Removal

**Equipment:** JLG G5-18A

1. Remove attachment from the quick coupler assembly. Refer to Operation and Safety Manual.
2. Be sure there is enough room in front of machine to allow boom sections to be removed.
3. Park the machine on a hard, level surface, place the transmission in (N) NEUTRAL, engage the park brake and shut the engine OFF.
4. Place a Do Not Operate Tag on both ignition key switch and steering wheel.
6. Open engine cover. Allow system fluids to cool.
8. Remove cylinder rod eye retaining clips and mounting pin ( 15 ) at side of second boom section.
