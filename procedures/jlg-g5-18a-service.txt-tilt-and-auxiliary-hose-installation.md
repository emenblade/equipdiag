# Tilt and Auxiliary Hose Installation

**Equipment:** JLG G5-18A

1. Connect the tilt cylinder hoses ( 12 ) and auxiliary hoses ( 13 ) to tilt cylinder/aux bulkhead.
2. Wrap new hoses under rear of second boom section ( 10 ).
3. Install the clamp ( 14 ) at the rear end of second boom section.
4. Tilt and Auxiliary Hose Installation
5. Support second boom section assembly.
6. Reposition straps, and guide second boom section into first boom section.
7. Reinstall extend/retract cylinder barrel pin ( 6 ).
8. Connect the tilt ( 4 ) and auxiliary ( 5 ) hoses at bottom of first boom section.
