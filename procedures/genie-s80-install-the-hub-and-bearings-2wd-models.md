# Install the Hub and Bearings, 2WD Models

**Equipment:** Genie S-80 / S-85

1. Be sure that both bearings are packed with clean, fresh grease.
2. Place the large inner bearing into the rear of the hub.
3. Press the bearing seal evenly into the hub until it is flush.
4. Slide the hub onto the yoke spindle. Component damage hazard. Do not apply excessive force or damage to the lip of the seal may occur.
5. Place the outer bearing into the hub.
6. Install the washer and castle nut.
8. Loosen the castle nut one turn, and then torque to 35 ft-lbs / 47 Nm.

> **Note:** When replacing a wheel bearing, both the inner and outer bearings including the pressed-in races must be replaced.

> **Note:** Rotate the hub by hand while torquing the castle nut to make sure the bearings seat properly.

> **Note:** Always use a new cotter pin when installing a castle nut.

> **Note:** When removing a hose assembly or fitting, the O-ring (if equipped) on the fitting and/or the hose end must be replaced.
