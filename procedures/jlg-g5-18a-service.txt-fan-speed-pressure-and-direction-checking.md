# Fan Speed Pressure and Direction Checking

**Equipment:** JLG G5-18A

1. Place a piece of paper over the face of the radiator core. If the paper is held in place, the fan is rotating in the right direction.
2. Attach a pressure gauge to the test fitting ( 6 ) at the rear of the implement pump.
3. If the machine is equipped with either the variable fan option or the variable, reversing fan option; run the engine at high idle and measure the fan speed using a suitable portable stroboscope adjustable to fractional RPM, and with the ability to distinguish actual RPM from harmonic frequencies. The fan speed must be a minimum of 2800 RPM at high idle.
4. Connect the   JLG   Analyzer and activate the Test Mode to drive the fan to the maximum fan RPM.
