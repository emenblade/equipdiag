# Remove the Hub and Bearings

**Equipment:** Genie S-80 / S-85

1. Loosen the wheel lug nuts. Do not remove them.
2. Block the non-steer wheels and center a lifting jack of ample capacity under the steer axle.
3. Raise the machine approximately 6 inches / 15 cm and place blocks under the chassis for support.
4. Remove the lug nuts. Remove the tire and wheel assembly.
6. Pull the hub off the spindle. The washer and outer bearing should fall loose from the hub.

> **Note:** Always replace the cotter pin with a new one when installing the castle nut.
