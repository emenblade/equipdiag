# Remove the Steer Cylinder

**Equipment:** Genie GS-1930 / GS-2632 / GS-3232

1. Block the non-steer tires.
2. Remove the pin retaining fasteners from the rod-end pivot pin. Remove the pivot pin.  Note: While removing the pin retaining fasteners, take note of the quantity and location of the spacers when removing the pivot pin.
3. Remove the pin retaining fasteners from the barrel-end pivot pin. Remove the pin.  Note: While removing the pin retaining fasteners, take note of the quantity and location of the spacers when removing the pivot pin.
4. Remove the steer cylinder from the machine.
5. Tag, disconnect and plug the hydraulic hoses from the steer cylinder. Cap the fittings on the cylinder.

> **Note:** When removing a hose assembly or fitting, the O-ring (if equipped) on the fitting and/or hose end must be replaced.

> **Note:** While removing the pin retaining fasteners, take note of the quantity and location of the spacers when removing the pivot pin.
