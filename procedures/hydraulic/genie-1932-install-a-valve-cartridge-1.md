# Install a Valve Cartridge

**Equipment:** Genie 1932 / 2032 / 2046

1. Dip the cartridge in clean oil to lube the O-rings.
2. Screw the cartridge in by hand until the top O-ring is met, then torque to specifications.
3. If required, install the valve coil(s) onto the valve stem. Install the coil nut onto the valve stem and torque to specifications.
4. Check valve, (GS-3232 and GS-3246 with load sense installed)   AK   Lift   23 ft-lbs / 31 Nm
5. Check disc   AD   Steer circuit   18 ft-lbs / 24 Nm
6. Solenoid valve, 2 position 4 way   AE   Drive speed select circuit   25 ft-lbs / 34 Nm
7. Relief valve   AF   Brake release   20 ft-lbs / 27 Nm
8. Solenoid valve, 3 position 5 way   AG   Drive forward/reverse   25 ft-lbs / 34 Nm
9. Flow regulator and relief valve, 0.75 gpm / 2.8 L/min, 1500 psi / 103 bar   AH   Steer circuit   26 ft-lbs / 35 Nm
10. Solenoid valve, 2 position 4 way   AI   Platform up   25 ft-lbs / 34 Nm
11. Relief valve   AJ   Lift relief   20 ft-lbs / 27 Nm
