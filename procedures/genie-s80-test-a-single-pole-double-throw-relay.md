# Test a Single Pole Double Throw Relay

**Equipment:** Genie S-80 / S-85

1. Tag and disconnect all the wiring from the relay to be tested.
2. Tag, disconnect and plug the hydraulic hoses from the function pump. Cap the fittings on the pump. Bodily injury hazard. Spraying hydraulic oil can penetrate and burn skin. Loosen hydraulic connections very slowly to allow the oil pressure to dissipate gradually. Do not allow oil to squirt or spray.
