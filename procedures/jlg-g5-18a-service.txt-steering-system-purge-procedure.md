# Steering System Purge Procedure

**Equipment:** JLG G5-18A

1. Start the machine. Warm the engine and hydraulic system to operating temperatures. Cycle all hydraulic functions several times to purge air from the system.
2. Place a Do Not Operate Tag on both ignition key switch and steering wheel.
3. Steering System Purge Procedure
4. Select   4-Wheel Circle Steer   ( 9 ) mode, and turn the steering wheel fully to the right, continuing a hard turn for 2 seconds after the stop to purge air from the system.
5. Remove the lower dash cover.
6. The rear wheels should be fully left. If not, remain in  2-Wheel Front Steer   ( 10 ) mode, and turn the steering wheel fully to the left, then repeat steps 4-6 before continuing.
7. Remove pin ( 13 ) and return spring securing service brake pedal to cab.
8. Select   2-Wheel Front Steer   ( 10 ) mode, and turn the steering wheel fully to the right, then fully to the left, continuing a hard turn for 2 seconds at the stops in each direction to purge air from the system. Repeat this cycle 2 times.
9. Remove a Do Not Operate Tag on both ignition key switch and steering wheel.  MY8860  13 12
