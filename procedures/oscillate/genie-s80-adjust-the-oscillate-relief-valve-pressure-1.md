# Adjust the Oscillate Relief Valve Pressure

**Equipment:** Genie S-80 / S-85

1. Remove the drive chassis cover from the non- steer end of the machine.
2. Connect a 0 to 2000 psi / 0 to 150 bar pressure gauge to the diagnostic nipple located near the oscillate directional valve.
3. Disconnect the directional valve linkage, by removing the clevis yoke from the drive chassis.
4. Start the engine from the ground control.
5. With the engine running, manually activate the valve and observe the pressure reading on the pressure gauge. Refer to Section 2, Specifications.
6. Turn the engine off.
7. Locate the relief valve on the directional valve. a   relief valve
8. Loosen the jam nut on the relief valve.
