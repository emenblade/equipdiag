# Activate the Battery Drain Alarm Option

**Equipment:** Genie GR-15 / GR-20

1. Pull out the red Emergency Stop button to the on position at the ground controls.
2. Push in the red Emergency Stop button to the off position at the platform controls.
3. Turn the key switch to platform control.
8. Press and hold the speed select button for a minimum of 3 seconds or until the 'ones' setting stops flashing.

> **Note:** To deactivate the battery drain alarm option on a machine which has the option activated, repeat this procedure.
