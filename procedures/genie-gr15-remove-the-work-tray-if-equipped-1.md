# Remove the Work Tray (if equipped)

**Equipment:** Genie GR-15 / GR-20

> **Electrocution/burn hazard.**
>
1. Turn the key switch to the off position.
2. Disconnect the battery pack from the machine.
3. Disconnect the platform controls coli cord, from the control cable at the platform.
4. Remove the fasteners and spacer securing the platform controls to the platform controls mount. Remove the platrom controls from the platform and set the controls to the side.
