# TIRES & WHEELS Tire Inflation

**Equipment:** JLG 800S / 860SJ

1. Start all nuts by hand to prevent cross threading. DO NOT use a lubricant on threads or nuts.
2. Remove the tire and wheel assembly.
3. The tightening of the nuts should be done in stages. Following the recommended sequence, tighten nuts per wheel torque chart.
4. Use a supporting device capable of handling the weight of the torque hub, drive brake, and drive motor

> **Note:** The   pneumatic   tire   &   wheel   assembly   weighs approximately 269 lbs.

> **Note:** The torque hub, drive brake, and drive motor assem- bly weighs approximately 275 lbs.
