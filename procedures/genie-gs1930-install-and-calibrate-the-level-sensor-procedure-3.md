11. Install the level sensor retaining fasteners through the level sensor and into the mount bracket.
12. Connect the chassis wire harness to the level sensor.
14. Push in the red Emergency Stop button to the off position at the ground controls.
15. Press and hold the ground control scroll up and scroll down buttons.  Ground Control Menu Buttons  1   scroll down button  2   enter button  3   scroll up button  4   escape button
17. Release the Scroll Up and Scroll Down buttons after the ground controller powers up.
18. Use the Scroll Up or Scroll Down buttons to scroll to   Select Option .
19. Press the Enter button.
20. Use the Scroll Up or Scroll Down buttons to scroll to Tilt Sensor.
21. Press the Enter button.
23. Raise the platform 7 to 8 feet / 2.1 to 2.4 m.
24. Return the safety arm to the stowed position.
25. Lower the platform to the stowed position.
26. Place a digital level on the ground control side of the drive chassis. Zero out the digital level.
29. Lower the platform to the stowed position.

> **Note:** If you are   not   installing a new level sensor, proceed to step 14.

> **Note:** Use a digital level to verify the surface is level.

> **Note:** The wire harness connection is located on the level sensor, on top of the chassis at the steer end of the machine.

> **Note:** The machine will not calibrate if it is on a slope of one degree or greater.

> **Note:** If the level sensor has been replaced, continue with step 23.
