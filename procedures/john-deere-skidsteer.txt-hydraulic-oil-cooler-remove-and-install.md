# Hydraulic Oil Cooler Remove and Install

**Equipment:** John Deere 326D / 328D / 329D / 332D / 333D

1. Remove front attachment and park machine on flat level surface.
2. Raise boom and engage boom lock.
3. Raise engine cover and remove right side panel. See Side Panel Remove and Install. (Group 1910.)
4. Remove cap screws (1) and move hydraulic oil filter assembly (3) out of way.
