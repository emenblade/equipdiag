# LSI Boom Sensors

**Equipment:** JLG G5-18A

1. Remove any fork carrier or attachment from the machine.
2. Park the machine on a firm, level surface, level the machine, fully retract the boom, fully raise the boom, place the transmission in (N) NEUTRAL, engage the park brake and shut the engine OFF.
3. LSI Boom Sensors  The LSI sensors ( 14 ) are bolted on the left inside of the first boom section.  a.   LSI Boom Sensor Removal
4. Open the battery and engine covers. Allow the engine to cool.
5. Close and secure the battery and engine covers.
6. Disconnect the LSI electrical connectors.
7. Loosen and remove the two bolts holding the LSI assembly to the side of the boom.
8. Remove and discard the sensor assembly.  b.   LSI Sensor Boom Installation
