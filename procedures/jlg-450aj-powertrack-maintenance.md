# POWERTRACK MAINTENANCE

**Equipment:** JLG 450AJ

> ⚠ **REPOSITION CABLES/HOSES AND KEEP COVERED DURING GRINDING TO PRE -  VENT DAMAGE.**
>
## MAINTENANCE

1. Squeeze cut-out end of new link into half-shear (female) end of track section.
2. Spread half-shear (female) end of new link and slide cut- out end of track section into it. Use a screwdriver if nec -  essary.
4. Pull moving end over track so new connection is posi -  tioned in curve of powertrack. Round half-shears will rotate into cut-outs.
5. Parts shown below connect new link to powertrack.
6. Insert flat head screwdriver between links. Twist and pull links apart.


> **Note:** Hoses shown in powertrack are for example only.

> **Note:** It may be necessary to loosen fixed end brackets from machine to move track section enough to disconnect links.

> **Note:** Make sure snap rings are seated in pin groove and closed properly.

> **Note:** When tightening screws make sure screw head is seated against link with no space in between link and underside of screw head.

> **Note:** When installing snap rings make sure they are seated in pin groove and closed properly.
