# Air Cleaner Remove and Install

**Equipment:** John Deere 326D / 328D / 329D / 332D / 333D

1. Remove front attachment and park machine on flat level surface.
2. Raise boom and engage boom lock.
3. Raise engine cover and remove side panels. See Side Panel Remove and Install. (Group 1910.)
4. Disconnect electrical connectors from air filter restriction switch (B1) and ambient air temperature sensor (B24).
5. Unscrew and remove air filter restriction switch (B1).
6. Loosen hose clamp (1).
7. Remove flange nuts from underside of air cleaner.
8. Remove air cleaner.
