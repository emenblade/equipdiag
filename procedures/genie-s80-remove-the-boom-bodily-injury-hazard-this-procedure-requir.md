# Remove the Boom

**Equipment:** Genie S-80 / S-85

1. Remove the platform. See 2-1, How to Remove the Platform.
2. Remove the platform rotator and leveling slave cylinder. See 2-3, How to Remove the Platform Rotator.
3. Remove the cable track. See 4-1, How to Remove the Cable Track.
5. Raise the boom to a horizontal position high enough that the boom lift cylinder rod end can clear the boom lift cylinder mounting boss.
6. Remove the turntable end cover.
7. Remove the pin retaining fasteners from the master cylinder rod-end pivot pin.
8. Place a rod through the pin and twist to remove the pin. Lower the rod end of the master cylinder.

> **Note:** Perform this procedure with the boom in the stowed position.

> **Note:** When removing a hose assembly or fitting, the O-ring (if equipped) on the fitting and/or the hose end must be replaced.
