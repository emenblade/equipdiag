# Quick Coupler Assembly Installation

**Equipment:** JLG G5-18A

1. Line up quick attach assembly between mounts on boom head. Quick attach assembly should be centered in boom head.
2. Quick Coupler Assembly Installation
3. Align quick coupler assembly with tilt cylinder rod end and insert tilt cylinder pin ( 3 ). Align tilt cylinder pin and quick coupler assembly. Install lock bolt ( 2 ). Torque to 72 lb-ft (97 Nm).
4. If equipped, uncap and reconnect hydraulic quick coupler assembly device hoses ( 1 ) to valve on left side of boom head.
5. Clean up all debris, hydraulic fluid, etc., in, on, near and around machine.
7. Close and secure engine cover.
8. Remove a Do Not Operate Tag on both ignition key switch and steering wheel.  3.8   FORKS  Forks should be cleaned and inspected prior to being attached to carriage. If the following criteria is not met, forks must be removed from service immediately.  Daily Inspection
9. BOOM PROP (IF EQUIPPED) 3.
