# JD Procedure

**Equipment:** John Deere 326D / 328D / 329D / 332D / 333D

1. Park machine.
2. Place safe lifting device under the front center of machine base and raise machine.
3. Place blocks/stands under the front corners (1) of machine base.
4. Lower machine onto blocks/stands.
5. Place safe lifting device under the rear center of machine base and raise machine.
6. Place blocks/stands under the rear corners (2) of machine base.
