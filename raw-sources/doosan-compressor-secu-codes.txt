Book 23307366 (09-08) Rev A   23  COMPRESSOR FAULT CODES DESCRIPTION AND TROUBLESHOOTING  Following, are the descriptions of the COMPRESSOR fault codes. These are indicated by the SECU Display Digit.
Book 23307366 (09-08) Rev A   24  COMPRESSOR CODE 1 S3, Discharge Temperature Switch Explanation:  The SECU has detected an open S3, compressor air discharge temperature Switch. Machine could be overheated or there could be a faulty switch or wiring.  Effect:  Code 1 will cause a machine shutdown  S3 Temperature Switch Circuit:  Circuit Description: S3 is the high temperature shutdown switch for the airend discharge temperature. The switch opens at 248 °   F. Component Location: S3 is located in the discharge pipe from the airend.  S3   SECU Controller 2 1 W006 Pink/Black W088 Red/Orange Splice E16 10
Book 23307366 (09-08) Rev A   25  TROUBLESHOOTING STEPS  Code 1  Action   Result  Step 1:  Check continuity of wire W006 form S3 pin 2 to SECU controller pin 10 Continuous connection  Step 2:  Check continuity of wire W088 from S3 pin 1 to splice E16 Continuous connection  Step3 :  Replace S3 switch Machine functions correctly
Book 23307366 (09-08) Rev A   26  COMPRESSOR CODE 2 S2, Separator Tank Temperature Switch Explanation:  The SECU has detected an open S2, compressor separator tank temperature Switch. Machine could be overheated or have a faulty switch or wiring.  Effect:  Code 2 will cause a machine shutdown  S2 Temperature Switch Circuit:  Circuit Description: S2 is the high temperature shutdown switch for the separator tank temperature. The switch opens at 248 °   F. Component Location: S2 is located in the side of the separator tank.  S2 W005 Pink/Blue Splice E16 SECU Controller 21 W087 Red/Orange 2 1
Book 23307366 (09-08) Rev A   27  TROUBLESHOOTING STEPS  Code 2  Action   Result  Step 1:  Check continuity of wire W005 from S2, pin 2 to SECU controller pin 21 Continuous connection  Step 2:  Check continuity of wire W087 from S2 to splice E16 Continuous connection  Step 3:  Replace S2 switch
Book 23307366 (09-08) Rev A   28  COMPRESSOR CODE 3 Low Alternator/Battery Voltage Explanation:  The SECU has detected a low alternator/battery voltage. It is below 11.6V. The alternator may not be charging the battery.  Effect:  Code 3 is an alert condition and will not shutdown the machine  Alternator Circuit:  Circuit Description: G1 is the engine mounted alternator. It connects to the starter solenoid via Wire WO78 and connects to the SECU pin 3 via wire W095, through Splice E17. Component Location: The alternator is mounted to the engine.  Alternator   W078 Red Case ground to engine +   Battery  F1   E14   S1   E1
Book 23307366 (09-08) Rev A   29  TROUBLESHOOTING STEPS  Code 3  Action   Result  Step 1:  Measure the voltage across the battery terminals with the engine running. Should be greater then 13.0 volts. If not, check alternator belt then goto Step 2.  Step 2:  Check continuity of wire W078 to starter. Check connections at battery and wire W095 to SECU If wiring faulty, replace, if not replace alternator
Book 23307366 (09-08) Rev A   30  COMPRESSOR CODE 5 S5, Engine Oil Pressure Switch Explanation:  The SECU has detected an open S5, engine oil pressure switch. Machine could have a loss of oil pressure.  Effect:  Code 5 will cause a machine shutdown  S5 Temperature Switch Circuit:  Circuit Description: S5 is the engine oil pressure switch. If the switch is open for 1 second while the engine is running, the SECU will shutdown the machine. Component Location: S5 is mounted on the side of engine block.  S5   SECU Controller 2 1 W008 Pink/Green W089 Red/Orange Splice E16 26
Book 23307366 (09-08) Rev A   31  TROUBLESHOOTING STEPS  Code 5  Check Oil Level  Action   Result  Step 1:  Check continuity of wire W008 form S5 pin 2 to SECU controller pin 26 Continuous connection  Step 2:  Check continuity of wire W089 from S3 pin 1o splice E16 Continuous connection  Step3 :  Replace S3 switch Machine functions correctly
Book 23307366 (09-08) Rev A   32  COMPRESSOR CODE 6 PT2, Regulation Pressure Transducer Explanation:  The SECU has detected an out of limits reading from the regulation transducer for 3 seconds while the machine is running.  Effect:  Code 6 will cause a machine shutdown  PS1 Transducer Circuit:  Circuit Description: PT2 is a pressure transducer used to read the regulation pressure. It is used by the SECU to control engine throttle. Component Location: PT2 is located in the inlet valve piping.  PT2 SECU Controller 1 2 W003 Brown 3 W002 Purple W001 Orange 31 35 6
Book 23307366 (09-08) Rev A   33  TROUBLESHOOTING STEPS  Code 6  Action   Result  Step 1:  Check for +5 VDC at pin 2 on PT2 5 VDC is correct power supply voltage  Step 2:  Check continuity of wire W001 to pin 6 on the SECU controller Continuous connection  Step3 :  Replace PT2 transducer Machine functions correctly
Book 23307366 (09-08) Rev A   34  COMPRESSOR CODE 8 Low Fuel Shutdown Explanation:  The SECU has detected an open switch at the optional U1 low fuel switch. This condition Will prevent engine cranking.  Effect:  Code 8 will cause a machine shutdown  U1 Transducer Circuit:  Circuit Description: The SECU controller senses the normally closed low fuel switch. Component Location: U1 is located in the fuel tank. If the machine does not have the optional fuel sensor, T3 and T4 are connected together, bypassing the fuel sensor switch.  U1 SECU Controller B A T4 Pink/Red T3 Red/Orange 34 Splice E16
Book 23307366 (09-08) Rev A   35  TROUBLESHOOTING STEPS  Code 8  Check Fuel Level  Action   Result  Step 1:  Disconnect U1 harness plug and remove U1 from fuel tank. Connect ohmmeter across pins A and B, Tilt U1 and ohmmeter should go from close to infinite ohms to near zero ohms. Shutdown switch in U1 is working correctly Check Connectors T3 and T4 Check W013 to SECU Pin 34 Check W090 to E16
Book 23307366 (09-08) Rev A   36  COMPRESSOR CODE 9 Air Filter Restriction Switch Explanation:  The SECU has detected a closed switch at the air filter restriction indicator.  Effect:  Code 9 is an alert condition and will not shutdown the machine.  Air Filter Restriction Circuit:  Circuit Description: Filter restriction switch is normally open and connects to the SECU controller. Component Location: Air filter restriction indicator is located on the air filter box.  Switch SECU Controller 2 1 W009 Dark Blue W091 Red/Orange 25 Splice E16
Book 23307366 (09-08) Rev A   37  TROUBLESHOOTING STEPS  Code 9  Action   Result  Step 1:  Replace air filter Alert goes away  Step2:  Check wire W009 continuity from filter restriction switch back to SECU controller. Replace if shorted to + 12 VDC.. No voltage at SECU controller
