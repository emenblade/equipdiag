M4H Device  CHECKS   Before troubleshooting, be sure the machine that M4H is installed on is moved/located outside. This gives the telematics device the best chance to establish proper CELL and GPS connectivity* !  * NOTE:   Some buildings and machine cabs can interfere with obtaining normal CELL and GPS connections. Prior to troubleshooting, ZTR recommends moving the machinery to a location outside of any building; provides best results.  What should I check when the M4H has stopped sending in location updates?  M4H Troubleshooting Reference    -> Is there any physical damage on the device itself? Check for cracks in the device housing Check for compromised seals -> Has the M4H been activated? Check with your account administrator to confirm the M4H has been properly activated -> Is the magnetic switch engaged? Confirm the magnet is removed from the rectangle landmark (see image at left) on the device -> Does the M4H have clear view of the sky? Ensure the M4H is not covered or installed inside a metal enclosure that shields it from the outside world. If so, relocate the device and test again  Have questions? Email UR Support: URSupport@ur.com  -> Is the M4H powering up correctly? Place a small magnet over the spot marked by the small rectangle on the top side of M4H for 10 seconds and remove it to cycle power to the unit  Magnet taped on = Power OFF Magnet exposed = Power Still OFF  Magnet removed = Power is ON  Magnet  Rectangle landmark for magnet
